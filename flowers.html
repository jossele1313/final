Wisteria- this flower grows from a tree that obviously this plant grows best in and around wet , moist soils. As pretty as this flower looks its actually toxic to animals but not humans.

meaning- despite this being a poisons flower it represents love and longevity, and to bring prosperity.




Red Spider Lilly- Fun fact the red spider Lilly is native to Japan, China, Korea and Nepal. this Flower likes to grow is shaded wet and rocky areas. It also grows near streams.

meaning- In Japan they believed that this flower represents souls passing through a void or purgatory after death.



Cherry Blossom- Ths flower come from a tree like the wisteria flower this flower also goes by the name the Sakura flower. unlike a regular flower or tree these are planted plants. They need lots of sunlight to grow successfully .

Meaning-this flower/ tree represents renewal, rebirth, and new beginings. Of course it also represents the start of spring.
